Reorder PDF Pages

Change the order of pages in your PDF

Click to Select PDF

Enter the page numbers in the order you want them. (e.g., To move page 3 to the front, type: 3, 1, 2...)

How to Reorder PDF Pages?

  1. Upload your PDF file using the box above.
  2. In the input box, type the new order of page numbers.
  3. For example, if your PDF has 3 pages and you want to reverse them, type: 3, 2, 1.
  4. Click the "Reorder & Download" button.
  5. Your new PDF with the rearranged pages will be ready instantly.

Organize Your Documents

Did you scan a document out of order? Use this tool to fix the sequence without expensive software. You can also use this to delete pages by simply omitting their numbers (e.g., typing "1, 3" will delete page 2).
