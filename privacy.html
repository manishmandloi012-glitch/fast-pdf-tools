Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

1. How we handle your files

We take your privacy seriously. Unlike other PDF tools, we do not upload your files to any server. All processing happens locally in your browser (Client-Side). Your files never leave your device.

Since we do not store your files, we do not collect any personal data or document contents. We may use third-party services like Google Analytics to track website usage statistics (e.g., number of visitors).

3. Cookies & Ads

We use third-party vendors, including Google, which use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings.
