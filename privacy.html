Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. How we handle your files

We take your privacy seriously. Unlike other PDF tools, we do not upload your files to any server. All processing happens locally in your browser (client-side). Your files never leave your device.

2. Data Collection

Since we do not store your files, we do not collect any personal data or document contents. We use Google Analytics to understand website traffic and usage patterns, such as which pages are visited and how often.

3. Cookies & Ads

We use third-party vendors, including Google, which use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites. You can view and manage Google's ad settings at adssettings.google.com, or disable cookies entirely in your browser settings.

4. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us.
