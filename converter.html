Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to convert images with sensitive information using this tool?

Yes. Your files are processed entirely inside your own browser using JavaScript. Nothing is uploaded to any server, so your images never leave your device.

Can I combine multiple images into one PDF?

Yes. You can select multiple JPG or PNG images, and each one becomes a separate page in a single PDF, in the order you select them.

What image formats are supported?

JPG and PNG images are supported. Other formats like WEBP or HEIC should be converted to JPG or PNG first.
