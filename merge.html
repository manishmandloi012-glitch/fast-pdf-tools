Why use Fast PDF Tools to merge PDFs?

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to merge PDF files with sensitive information using this tool?

Yes. Your files are processed entirely inside your own browser using JavaScript. Nothing is uploaded to any server, so your documents never leave your device.

Is there a limit on how many PDF files I can merge?

There is no fixed limit set by the tool. The practical limit depends on your device's memory, since merging happens locally in your browser rather than on a server.

Will merging PDFs reduce the quality of my documents?

No. This tool copies the original pages directly into the new file without re-compressing or re-rendering them, so the quality stays exactly the same as your source files.
