Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to convert Word documents with sensitive information using this tool?

Yes. Your file is read and converted entirely inside your own browser using JavaScript. Nothing is uploaded to any server, so your document never leaves your device.

Does this support old .doc files?

No, only the modern .docx format is supported. If you have an older .doc file, open and re-save it as .docx in Word first.

Will the PDF look exactly like my Word document?

Text, headings, lists, and basic formatting are preserved. Complex layouts such as text boxes, precise page breaks, or heavily customized tables may not match the original exactly.
