How to Rotate PDF Pages?

  1. Upload your PDF file by clicking the box above.
  2. Select the rotation angle (90°, 180°, or 270°).
  3. Click the "Rotate & Download" button.
  4. The new file with the corrected orientation will download immediately.

Why use this tool?

Scanned documents are often upside down or sideways. Our Rotate PDF tool fixes this permanently.

  • Permanent Rotation: Unlike some viewers that only rotate the view, we save the file with the new orientation.
  • Privacy First: Your documents are processed in your browser, not on a server.
