Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to rotate PDF files with sensitive information using this tool?

Yes. Your files are processed entirely inside your own browser using JavaScript. Nothing is uploaded to any server, so your documents never leave your device.

Can I rotate just one page instead of the whole document?

This tool rotates all pages in the document by the same angle. If you need to rotate individual pages differently, you may need a more advanced editor.

Will rotating a PDF reduce its quality?

No. Rotation only changes the page orientation metadata and does not re-render or re-compress the content, so quality is fully preserved.
