Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to compress PDF files with sensitive information using this tool?

Yes. Your files are processed entirely inside your own browser using JavaScript. Nothing is uploaded to any server, so your documents never leave your device.

How much can I reduce my PDF's file size?

It depends on the file. PDFs with large embedded images usually shrink the most, since compression mainly reduces image quality and removes unnecessary embedded data. Text-heavy PDFs with few images will shrink much less.

Will compressing reduce my PDF's quality?

Compression works by reducing embedded image quality slightly to save space. Text stays sharp, but images may show minor quality loss depending on the compression level you choose.
