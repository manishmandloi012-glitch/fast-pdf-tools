Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to split PDF files with sensitive information using this tool?

Yes. Your files are processed entirely inside your own browser using JavaScript. Nothing is uploaded to any server, so your documents never leave your device.

Can I extract just a few specific pages instead of splitting the whole file?

Yes. You can choose which pages to extract into a new PDF, rather than splitting every page into its own file.

Will splitting a PDF reduce its quality?

No. Pages are copied directly from the original file without re-compressing or re-rendering them, so quality is fully preserved.
